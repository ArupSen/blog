  • Diet

    Goals: Focus on what to eat rather than what to avoid i.e. more fibre, veg, oily fish.

    I had pizza, beer and also chips. Lots of chocolate too. Oh and biscuits. So yeah, all very healthy.

    As the month has progressed I have been watching many YouTube videos about diet and what to eat and what not to eat. Even though I know that it would be futile and short lived to attempt any form of healthy eating regime, I did enjoy very much thinking about implementing these ideas. I've been trying to convince a friend to take up the 5:2 fasting diet. He sees that it would be beneficial but does not believe that he could keep it up. I've researched other forms of intermittent fasting and found a recent trend to follow a protocol known as 16:8. These refer to hours rather than days. Instead of fasting for two out of the seven days of the week the idea was to fast every day and inside of a 24 hour period 16 of those hours were fasting and 8 were eating. It was referred to as a fasting window and feeding window. Due to the feeding window being so short and having to consume the required calories inside an 8 hour period it was also called a feasting window. My friend thought that this would be something that he could actually maintain and considered that it was more within his reach than the 5:2 protocol. I want to lose bodyfat myself and so am considering that I add it to my current 5:2 regime.
