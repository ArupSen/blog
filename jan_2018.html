It's the 2nd of January 2018 and I haven't yet formulated a year plan. But I'm not going to let that stop me from starting to work on my goals. I have a fairly clear idea of what I want so here goes.

So far so good with week one. I feel very enthused about it all but still need to write out the year plan properly and also to complete the 2017 review.

Now into week 2 on Friday 12th it does feel that I'm on track. The main thing is that I have fully recovered from whatever I had during most of December and I feel that my energy and motivation have returned.

Week 3 went pretty well and I was very focused on my set of goals partly because they are fresh and new as it's the new year and also that I've been working on my 2017 review on and off throughout January.

  • Clutter

    Goals: Sell one item on eBay. Discard one item from each room.

    I've put 2 items on eBay and threw out a load of paperwork which felt really good.

    During the first week of January I have been thinking about getting a bigger screen. I got quite obsessive about it and have also considered a projector. But when I sat down to think about what was prompting it all I realised that it was all about removing clutter. What I want is to remove my desk and office chair and use the computer from my sofa as I do with gaming and watching movies. Obviously when you have a new idea and get obsessive about it you want to it immediately. But I've fallen into that trap all too often. I don't want to be a buzzkill but I want to see if it's a one night stand or something more serious. Getting rid of my desk and chair would be a big change because I tend to do all of my computer related work with that set up. I used to also use my laptop and that would still be an option but to use my main computer I would have to use it without a desk with the keyboard in my lap. So I'm going to give it a couple of weeks, say till the 20th and then see if I still like the idea.

    The first item that I listed, a doorbell, didn't sell so I have re-listed it and have also listed a third item. Fingers crossed. It's good just to be aware of what I have that definitely needs to go and others that could potentially be discarded.

    By the time the 20th of the month came around I found that I had not missed my desk at all and decided to rearrange the sitting room to make it desk free and also create a bit more space. It feels more spacious and has more of a sense of being a sitting room rather than an office. The speakers were too close to the monitor which gave a sense of clutter and now that they are further apart there seems to be a better soundstage, almost a surround sound feel. It was a very satisfying weekend DIY project to do while it was raining outside.

    As well the sitting room changes and declutting I added many things to eBay and cleared out various things from shelves and cupboards. I've also decided to get rid of all of my DVD collection. These days I tend to stream rather than put a DVD disc into the computer so they really are redundant.

  • Sleep

    Goals: Lights out by 00:30. Read rather than watch before bed.
